AI CHORD/CHORD PROGRESSION GENERATOR

GOALS:
- Django web app to generate music chords from a user prompt
- Trained on jazz, classical, pop, rock, hip-hop, electronic music to be able to generate a large variety of chord progressions.
- Natural lanuage processing prompts - "generate a sad jazzy chord progression in D minor", "give me a happy sounding pop chord", conversational - "what chord could sound good after that one?"

Steps:
1. Get data:
	- Use MIDI files to train AI.
	- Find chords in MIDI
	- Represent the chords as symbols - Cm7, Dmaj9#11
